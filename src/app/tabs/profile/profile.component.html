¡Where it all began!
In the early days of the pandemic, when the world came to a halt, I found myself scrolling through the endless void of the internet. Suddenly, a word caught my attention: "Android Studio". Intrigued, I downloaded it and started tinkering with the demo app. Hours flew by as I delved deeper into the world of app development. When it was time for lunch, I couldn't bear to tear myself away from my newfound passion.
And when dinnertime rolled around, I realized I had skipped a meal entirely! That's when I knew I had found something special. As I looked back to my days as a computer science student, co-founding a startup to digitize local businesses, I realized that my old passion had been reignited.
¡Where I stand now!
