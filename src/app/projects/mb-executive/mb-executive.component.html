mbExecutive
Financial Dashboard & Reports
Progressive Web App
January 2021
This PWA combines multiple sources of data to provide a complete and accurate financial picture through interactive tables and charts highlighting main KPIs, using real-time data within any range of dates.
MB-Tech hired me with the goal to develop a new app which could provide remote access to the data of one of their major desktop apps. I was trusted with the full responsibility of both Front and Backend. Understanding the problem, UX requirements and reusability was discussed remotely to define the goals.
A happy and bold color theme is well combined with numbers to show the daily routine information in just a single view on dashboard. Splash screen, resolver and singleton services makes possible a smooth landing and good UX.
Enhanced Business reporting with real-time information.
It puts the power of sophisticated data analytics for advanced forecasting, budgeting and planning directly into the hands of executives or financial analysts.
