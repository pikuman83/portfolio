¡Where it all began!
In the early days of the pandemic, totally locked down, one morning while surfing the web, a word, "Android Studio", got my attention. I downloaded it. While playing with the demo app, Suddenly, it was lunch time and I DIDN'T want to eat.
What happened? I had no idea. Still playing with the app
now it's dinner time and I'm still not hungry. What's going on here?
To get the answer, I had to travel 22 years back in time, when while studying Computer Science, together with a friend, we created a startup to digitize local companies, which I left when I moved to Barcelona.
!! Suddenly I realised, it was the reawakening of my old passion!!
¡Where I stand now!
The start-up, "MB-TECH", is still alive and continuing to offer solutions to local companies. However, back at the beginning of the pandemic, it was in need of innovation. So I took up the challenge to remake one of their VB based major apps (a very complete ERP) as a PWA, details of which can be found in the "Projects" tab.
Although I've been learning by doing, I've been able to work as a full stack developer at a decent pace on this project.
